Wind and Shine Site Plan

Purpose:

Wind and Shine is a weather site that will contain weather information in a variety of formats. The weather presented will be in a dynamically allocated format. The purpose of the site is to provide the users with accurate weather information for a varriety of locations on a varriety of devices. We strive to make sure our users have access to the information that they will need. Our home page will show the user the weather at their current location. The 'Favorites' page will show the users the weather of the last five locations visited. We will use images to depict the weather conditions, as well as the town chosen by the user.
